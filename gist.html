Crafting a presentation can be hard

Too many tools

  • Keynote
  • ShowOff
  • PowerPoint
  • PDFs
  • etc

Too many options

  • Templates
  • Clip Art
  • Screen Sizes
  • Giant Laptops
  • Little People

Too many distractions

  • Toolbars
  • Sidebars
  • Drawing tools
  • Windows
  • Lolcats

Why can't it be easier?

Why can't we have less interface? Why can't we use tools we're familiar with - tools we use every day? Why can't we publish instantly? Why can't we instantly deploy our presentations?
